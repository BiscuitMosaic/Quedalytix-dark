About us

Quedalytix offers businesses the possibility to improve and align with current digital trends, by using technology tools to boost their strategical KPI and adapt to global market challenges. Founded in 2005 in Bucharest, Quedalytix is a European innovative group of tech companies. Over the years, the company re-invented several times, passed through different stages and gained robustness and maturity. 2016 marked the first steps of regional expansion by opening offices in Belgrade – Serbia and Sofia – Bulgaria.
